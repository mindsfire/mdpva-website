About the Association

A guild of working professionals, rooted in the cultural capital of Karnataka.

From palace weddings and Dasara processions to the forests of Nagarahole, MDPVA members document the life of Mysuru district. We set professional standards, run training and certification, and connect clients with verified photographers and videographers.
